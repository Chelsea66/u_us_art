章魚Ｘ壽司

This section of my artist book features a diverse array of sea creatures, including an octopus, shrimps, a frog, and three lantern fish. The story follows the journey of the shrimps as they swim from the octopus's sucker into the vast ocean, only to end up as delicious shrimp sushi. Along the way, we witness the stunning stalactites and stalagmites on the octopus's palms, symbolizing the masked life we led during the Covid-19 quarantine. And in a thought-provoking twist, the appearance of an octopus-frog hybrid catching a dragonfly with its tongue, while a small frog watches on, highlights the idiom, "The mantis stalks the cicada, unaware the oriole is behind." This story is a reflection on the beauty and complexities of the natural world, with subtle nods to our own human experiences.
